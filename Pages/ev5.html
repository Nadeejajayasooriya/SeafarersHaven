Ship Energy Efficiency.

Safe Cargo Handling-FRAMO  

 8:30AM-4:30PM Venue At Seafarers Haven

A session on Safe Cargo Handling – FRAMO will be held to familiarize seafarers with the operation and safety measures of FRAMO cargo pumping systems. Heavy Weather Damage-Container Vessel. 

 8:30AM-4:30PM Venue At Seafarers Haven

A session on Heavy Weather Damage – Container Vessel will be conducted to prepare seafarers for managing and minimizing risks during rough sea conditions. About Us

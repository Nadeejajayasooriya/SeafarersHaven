2025
 

Mooring.

 8.30AM-4.00PM Venue At Seafarers Haven

A session on Mooring will be conducted to train seafarers in the safe and effective handling of mooring operations. The training will cover line handling, winch operation, tension control, and hazard awareness during berthing and unberthing. It aims to prevent accidents, ensure crew safety, and support smooth docking procedures in various port conditions.

About Us

Seafarers Haven, built by seafarers for seafarers, is dedicated to creating a welcoming space for mariners to find rest and support. We aim to enhance their well-being, celebrate their contributions, and build a global community that values their dedication.
